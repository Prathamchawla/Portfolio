Heat Map

->Heat maps are commonly used to visualize the correlation matrix of variables in a dataset.

->They provide a visual representation of the strength and direction of relationships between different variables

Histogram Plots

->Histograms provide a visual representation of the distribution of a single variable, showing the frequency or count of values within different intervals or bins.

-> By examining the shape of the histogram, one can gain insights into the central tendency (mean, median) and spread (range, variability) of the data.
